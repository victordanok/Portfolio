About Me

Hi, I am Victor and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Science (Mechanical Engineering, University of Lagos). I worked in the operations department of a Nationwide logistics company, where I got exposed to unstuctured data and I had to make sense of it in order to prepare timely reports. My passion for working with data led me to explore other more efficient ways to collect and organize data outside of Microsoft Excel.

Due to my passion, I retrained in data science with Stutern. During this training, I personally worked on the projects displayed on this website and I have come to discover that getting insights out of data is something I enjoy doing and I use data visualization and machine learning techniques to achieve this.
